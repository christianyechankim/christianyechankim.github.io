ELIGIBILITY FOR APPLICATION

  • Faithful Christian pastors or pastor candidates who have the language ability to complete college-level education.


  • Those who want to plant a church after graduation or devote their passion and devotion to church ministry.


  • Those who need education in connection with other theological educational institutions after graduation.


  • UNEV bachelor's degree course requires a high school diploma and certain visa documents.


  • The B.E.S.T. course has priority for pastors, but anyone can apply.
